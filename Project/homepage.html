ABOUT THANKS A LATTE

The dramatic increase in demand for coffee has created the rapid expansion of production—shifting the industry from traditional, sustainable coffee growing methods to intense monoculture models that require massive amounts of fertilizer and pesticides, decrease biodiversity, and deplete the soil. So what’s an eco-minded coffee addict to do?
We always look for 100 percent Arabica varieties to ensure both higher quality coffee and better standards for the environment. Whenever possible, we purchase coffee products made as close to home as possible. This way we cut down on the fossil fuel required to ship coffee products and help the environment in the process. Supporting local coffee roasters also helps sustain local economies. In addition, we also exclusively only use brewing devices that don’t require electricity—including French presses, Chemex, and Aeropress—use, which all use less energy than their electric counterparts. We also choose to use a cold-brew coffee maker, which doesn’t require any heat to boil water.
